- **NOTE**
    
    ---
    
    - Always round *up* (ex. 6.23 → 7)
    - `[]` → includes
        
        `()` → does not include 
        
        (ex. `[0, 5)` includes 0, 1, 2, 3, 4;
        
         `[5, 10)` includes 5, 6, 7, 8, 9;
        
         `[10, 15]` includes 10, 11, 12, 13, 14, 15)
