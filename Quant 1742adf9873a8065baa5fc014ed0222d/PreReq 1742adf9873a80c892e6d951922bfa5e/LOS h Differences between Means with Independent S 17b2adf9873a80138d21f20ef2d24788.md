### **Interpretation $H_a$**

---

- 2-sided
    - $\mu_1 \neq \mu_2$
- 1-sided
    - either, $\mu_1 > \mu_2$ (right)
    - or, $\mu_1 < \mu_2$ (left)

---

$H_0$ represents everything that remains
