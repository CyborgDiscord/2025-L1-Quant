### NOTE

---

**In e.g./**

- **Odds** = 1 to 7 *(expressed as **a to b**)*
- **Probability** = $\frac{\text{a}}{\text{a+b}}$ = $\frac{\text{1}}{\text{1+7}}$ = $\frac{\text{1}}{\text{8}}$
